Welcome to Fruitimon!

Fruitimon is a totally fun card game that is totally original and that totally was not inspired by any other game, ever. Totally. Players can collect and trade cards which they then can use to battle each other in Fruitimon Duels. Each card has a Fruitimon: a plant/animal hybrid each with their own unique powers and abilities. Each card lists the Fruitimon's stats, abilities, and a small description of that Fruitimon. These Fruitimon are the weapons that a player uses to demonstrate their superiority on the battlefield and claim the title of Fruitimon Champion. To learn more about how to play Fruitimon, check out the Play page, and to look at some featured Fruitimon, see the Fruits page. Browse the News to hear all of the new features and updates for the game, and browse the Shop to find out how you can get Fruitimon for yourself!
